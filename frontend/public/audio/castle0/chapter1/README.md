# Castle 7 - Chapter 1 Audio Files
## The Trial Grounds - Pretest Assessment

### Opening Dialogue Audio
These audio files correspond to the opening dialogue in the pretest assessment:

1. **opening_1.wav** - "Welcome, brave adventurer! Before you embark on your journey through the Kingdom of Geometry..."
2. **opening_2.wav** - "The Guardian must assess your current knowledge."
3. **opening_3.wav** - "Don't worry - there are no wrong answers here. This helps us understand what you already know!"
4. **opening_4.wav** - "Are you ready to begin the Trial?"

### Audio Format
- Format: WAV
- Sample Rate: 44.1kHz recommended
- Channels: Mono or Stereo
- Bit Depth: 16-bit recommended

### Notes
- All audio files should be recorded with a friendly, encouraging tone
- Keep background music subtle to maintain focus on the narration
- File naming follows zero-indexed convention for consistency with other castles
